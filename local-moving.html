Local Moving Service Options, Customized to Your Needs

Whether you just need a few furniture pieces moved or a full-service relocation, Super Good Movers can provide any local moving service necessary for a seamless relocation. Super Good Movers is one of the few moving companies with the expertise to provide specialized and high end relocation services. Our clients can customize their move to utilize as many or few of Super Good Movers service options as they please.

What to Expect When You Move Locally with Super Good Movers

Step 1: Start planning with a free moving consultation and estimate

Start by requesting a free, no obligation moving estimate. Every move is unique, and understanding your needs is key to a successful relocation. A move adviser will meet with you, either in your home or via phone, to thoroughly discuss your plans, needs, and inventory in order to provide you with an accurate pricing estimate.

Step 2: Communicate with your dedicated move coordinator and online portal

When you’re ready to choose Super Good Movers, reach out to your move coordinator to reserve your dates. Personalized communication is necessary for good service. Every family is assigned a dedicated coordinator to answer questions and guide preparations for move day. In addition to your coordinator’s calls to make sure you’re ready for move day, you can view and manage your move details, download documents, and more online via your Super Good Movers Move Portal.

Step 3: Prepare to move with our complimentary resources

Get packing and order moving boxes with free delivery right to your door. Utilize the hundreds of moving tips, how-to videos, and free downloads in the Super Good Movers Move Planning Center. Use Super Good Movers address change tool to update USPS, subscriptions, and utilities. Need to make a change or last minute packing help? Reach out to your coordinator and they will make sure you’re covered.

Step 4: Your professional moving crew arrives

The moment you meet your team of Super Good Movers movers, you will be at ease knowing a team of friendly professionals will take care of your every need. The move will begin with a walk through of your home to review the day’s plan.

Step 5: Home and furniture protection

Our crew will use home protection kits to keep floors and walls damage-free, and shrink or pad-wrap furniture to keep your belongings in perfect condition. Your crew leader is trained to make decisions to complete your move efficiently and with care, including any necessary furniture disassembly, maneuvering, or hoisting.

Step 6: Welcome home

Our crew will place your furniture exactly where you want it in your new home. They will complete a walk through with you at the conclusion of the move to ensure satisfaction with the service.

Step 6: How did we do?

We will be in touch after the move to make sure you were happy with the service, tie up any loose ends, and ask if there’s anything we can improve.
